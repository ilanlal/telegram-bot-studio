# Demo Video Spec: Google Verification Process

**Total Duration:** 32 seconds  
**Split:** 4 subclips, 8 seconds each

---

## Overview

This video demonstrates the Google Sheets add-on "Telegram Bot Studio" for verification purposes. It is divided into four 8-second subclips, each focusing on a key aspect of the add-on and its required OAuth scopes.

---

## Subclip 1: Welcome & Introduction (0-8s)

**On-Screen Actions:**

- Google Sheets is open.
- Welcome screen or overlay appears: "Welcome to Telegram Bot Studio Add-on".
- Brief animation or highlight of the add-on icon in the sidebar.

**Visual Cues:**

- Welcome message centered on screen.
- Telegram Bot Studio icon in the sidebar is highlighted.

**Narration:**
> "Welcome to Telegram Bot Studio for Google Sheets. This add-on lets you connect and manage your Telegram bots directly from your spreadsheet."

---

## Subclip 2: Navigating to the Add-on (8-16s)

**On-Screen Actions:**

- Locate and click the Telegram Bot Studio icon directly in the sidebar.
- Sidebar appears with the Telegram Bot Studio interface loading.

**Visual Cues:**

- Highlight/callout on the Telegram Bot Studio icon in the sidebar.
- Sidebar area is briefly outlined as it loads.

**Narration:**
> "Open the add-on by clicking the Telegram Bot Studio icon in the sidebar. The interface loads, ready for you to connect your bot."

---

## Subclip 3: Connect Bot & Save Info (16-24s)

**On-Screen Actions:**

- In the sidebar, navigate to the bot connection/token setup screen.
- Paste a Telegram bot token into the input field.
- Click the "Connect" button.
- Show a success message or green checkmark indicating a successful connection.
- Animation shows the bot info being saved into the current spreadsheet (e.g., a new row appears with bot details).

**Visual Cues:**

- Highlight the token input field and the "Connect" button.
- Tooltip: "Your Telegram bot token is required to connect."
- Highlight the spreadsheet area where bot info is saved.

**Narration:**
> "Paste your Telegram bot token and connect. The add-on uses the 'script.external_requests' scope to communicate with Telegram, and saves your bot info securely in the current spreadsheet using the 'spreadsheets.currentonly' scope."

---

## Subclip 4: Verification Summary (24-32s)

**On-Screen Actions:**

- Show a summary overlay listing the key verification steps completed.
- Visual: Checkmarks next to each step (e.g., "Opened Add-on", "Connected Bot", "Saved Info").
- Brief animation: Spreadsheet and Telegram icons connected by a line.

**Visual Cues:**

- Emphasize completion with green checkmarks.
- Overlay text: "Verification Complete!"

**Narration:**
> "Verification is complete. You've opened the add-on, connected your Telegram bot, and saved your info securely in Google Sheets."
