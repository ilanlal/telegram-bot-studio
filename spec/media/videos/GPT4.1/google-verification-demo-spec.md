# Google Verification Demo Video Spec

**Total Duration:** 24 seconds  
**Segments:** 3 (8 seconds each)

---

## Segment 1 (0-8s): Navigating to the Add-on in Google Sheets

- **Visuals:**
  - Open Google Sheets in a browser.
  - Go to the "Extensions" menu.
  - Select "Telegram Bot Studio" from the add-ons list.
  - Open the add-on sidebar/panel.
- **On-screen text:**  
  - "Step 1: Open Google Sheets and launch the Telegram Bot Studio add-on."
- **Voiceover (optional):**  
  - "Start by opening Google Sheets and launching the Telegram Bot Studio add-on from the Extensions menu."

---

## Segment 2 (8-16s): Setting Up a Bot Connection

- **Visuals:**
  - In the add-on sidebar, click "Connect Bot".
  - Paste a Telegram bot token.
  - Click "Connect" or "Save".
  - Show a success message or connected status.
- **On-screen text:**  
  - "Step 2: Connect your Telegram bot by entering your bot token."
- **Voiceover (optional):**  
  - "Next, connect your Telegram bot by entering your bot token and confirming the connection."

---

## Segment 3 (16-24s): Demonstrating Scope Usage (`spreadsheets.currentonly`)

- **Visuals:**
  - Trigger an action in the add-on that writes data to the current sheet (e.g., send a message from Telegram and show it appear in the sheet).
  - Highlight the updated cell or row.
  - Briefly show the permissions dialog, emphasizing 'spreadsheets.currentonly'.
- **On-screen text:**  
  - "Step 3: The add-on writes to your current sheet only, as required by its permissions."
- **Voiceover (optional):**  
  - "The add-on uses the 'spreadsheets.currentonly' scope to write data only to the active sheet, ensuring your data privacy."
